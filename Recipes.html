Recipes - World's Best Scrambled Eggs

I adapted this recipe from a book called Sydney Food by Bill Grainger. Ever since tasting these eggs on my 1st visit to Bill's restaurant in Kings Cross, Sydney. I have been after the recipe I have sinci transformed it into what I really believe are the best scrambled eggs I have ever tasted

This recipe is what I call a very special breakfast lust look at the ingredients to see why It has to be tasted to be believed

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter(10g)
  • 1.3 cup of cream
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly milled black pepper
  • 3 fresh chives(chopped)

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, cream. and salt m a bow
  2. Melt the butter in a non-stick pan over a high heat (talane care not to burn the butter)
  3. Pour egv mixture into pan and wait until it starts setting around the edge of the pan (around 20 seconds).
  4. Using a wooden spatula. bring the mixture into the center as if it were an omelet. and let it cook for another 20 seconds.
  5. Fold contents in again, leave for 20 seconds. and repeat until the eggs are only Just done
  6. Grind a light sprinkling of freshly milled pepper over the eggs and blend in sonic chopped flesh chives

You should only make a maximum of two servings per frying pan
